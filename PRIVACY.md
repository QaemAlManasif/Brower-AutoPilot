# Privacy Policy — Browser Autopilot

**Last updated:** June 2026

Browser Autopilot ("the extension") is built with privacy as a core principle.

## What we collect
We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data, browsing history, or page content.

## What's stored, and where
All recordings, automation steps, settings, variables, and run history are stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's `storage.local` API. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## License activation (Pro)
If you activate a Pro license, your license key is sent to our payment processor, Polar (polar.sh), solely to verify and activate your license. No browsing data, recordings, or automation content is ever included in this request.

## Permissions
- **storage** — to save your automations and settings locally.
- **activeTab / scripting / tabs** — to record and replay your actions on the pages you choose to automate.
- **Host access (all sites)** — required because automations can be created for any website you visit; this access is used only to run the actions you've recorded, never to monitor or log your browsing.

## Data sharing
We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

## Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
